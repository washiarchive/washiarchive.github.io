Washi primer: fibers & sheet formation
Published: Oct 18, 2025 · background

Washi is traditionally made from kozo (paper mulberry), gampi, and mitsumata. Key steps: fiber prep, beating, suspension with neri (formation aid), sheet formation by agitation, pressing, and drying. These factors influence fiber morphology visible in macro images, aiding attribution and conservation decisions.

See the Core Dataset (DOI) for standardized metadata fields used in the archive.
