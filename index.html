Digital Washi Archive

Companion page to the Harvard Dataverse collection. DOI-citable datasets of historic Japanese paper (washi) with macro images & standardized metadata.

Open Dataverse Collection Start Here — Core Dataset DOI Access the Blog Background

Why this matters

To my knowledge, this is the only dedicated washi repository on Harvard Dataverse (as of Oct 16, 2025), enabling citable reuse by conservation, design, and DH communities.

Methods

Macro imaging pipeline (lens & lighting), standardized metadata (fiber, region, maker/period, usage), provenance & rights notes. See the “Start Here” dataset for details.

Keywords

Washi; Japanese paper; Kozo; Gampi; Mitsumata; Handmade paper; Paper conservation; Bast fibers; Fiber analysis; Macro photography.
